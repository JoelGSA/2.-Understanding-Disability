Understanding Disability, Inclusion and Accessibility

This course builds your understanding of disability, inclusion, and accessibility from the ground up. Across seven modules you'll explore how disability is understood and experienced, learn practical strategies for inclusive communication and design, and develop the skills to contribute to a genuinely inclusive workplace.

Module 1
The context of disability

This module looks at how disability is understood across different social contexts, and how attitudes and environments shape the experiences of people with disability.

  • The context of disability
  • What we have learned
Module 2
The models of disability

This module covers the key models of disability and how they influence the way disability is understood and responded to. You'll apply these models to real-life scenarios.

  • Models of disability
  • Scenarios
Module 3
Disability types

This module covers the different types of disability and how they can affect people in different ways. You'll also explore intersectionality and how overlapping identities can shape a person's experience.

  • Disability types
  • Intersectionality
Module 4
Accessibility and inclusion

This module explores what accessibility and inclusion mean in practice, and why they matter for creating fair environments. You'll also look at how hardware and software can support accessibility.

  • Accessibility
  • Inclusion
  • Hardware and software
Module 5
Universal design

This module introduces the 7 Principles of Universal Design and how they can be used to create inclusive spaces and experiences, including key touchpoints in the workplace.

  • The 7 principles of universal design
  • Workplace touchpoints
Module 6
Inclusive language and communication

This module covers how to use inclusive language and communicate respectfully with people with disability, with practical strategies you can apply straight away.

  • Using inclusive language and communication
  • Inclusive language
Module 7
Inclusive teams

This module focuses on what it takes to build an inclusive team. You'll look at real employee scenarios and explore how inclusive communication and collaboration make a difference.

  • Inclusive communication: employees
  • Employee scenarios
